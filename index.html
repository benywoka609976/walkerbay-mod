A Letter from
The Founder

"Welcome to Walkerbay Hotels – Muhuru Bay’s Premier Hospitality Destination Walkerbay Hotels is a modern boutique hotel located in the serene lakeside town of Muhuru Bay, Migori County, overlooking the beautiful shores of Lake Victoria. We provide a perfect blend of comfort, elegance, and warm Kenyan hospitality—making every guest feel at home whether visiting for business, leisure, or family travel. The hotel comprises of well-appointed guest rooms and suites, restaurant,bars, 24-hour In-Room Dining service, Walkerbay Hotel provides the perfect venue for meetings and events with more than 1500 sqm of meeting and banquet space, perfect for seminars, large conferences, cocktail events and corporate dinners, an ideal mix of categories to meet the needs of corporate and MICE visitors to the country. The property offers uninterrupted views of Lake Victoria and Mwarache- Hills."

Victor
